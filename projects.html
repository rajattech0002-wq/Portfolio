SmartHeart — Automated Cardiac MRI Planning

Philips Research

Developed core components of an AI-driven solution that automates cardiac MRI planning by selecting optimal heart views from low-resolution 3D survey images.

  • Key Achievement: 50% time savings in scan preparation, reducing operator dependence
  • Pipeline: Built and integrated four-model system:
    • Heart Mask (heart localization, ROI extraction, shim volume)
    • IQ Assessment (survey quality monitoring)
    • Landmark (anatomical landmark detection)
    • Plane Prediction (cardiac plane segmentation)
  • Business Impact: Expected to generate XM EUR over five years at YK EUR per license
  • Contributions: PDLM milestones, BU innovation roadmap planning, RSNA 2025 deliverables
  • Recognition: Project publicly highlighted on LinkedIn Video

SmartSpine — Automated Spine MRI Planning

Philips Research

Developing an AI-driven solution that automates MRI spine planning across cervical, thoracic, lumbar, and combined 3D surveys.

  • Core Model: nnU-Net based segmentation model for spinal anatomy localization from 3D MR surveys with high accuracy
  • Features: Generates multiple 3D planning boxes enabling automated sagittal and axial prescription
  • Impact: Reduces manual planning effort and improves workflow consistency and operator-independence
  • Integration: Advanced capabilities integrated into broader MR automation roadmap alongside SmartHeart
  • Coverage: Supports cervical, thoracic, lumbar, and combined (C, T, C+T, C+L+T) surveys
